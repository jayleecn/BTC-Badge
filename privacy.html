Privacy Policy for BTC Badge - Bitcoin Price Ticker

Last updated: August 8, 2024

1. Information Collection and Use

BTC Badge does not collect any personal information from its users. The extension only fetches publicly available Bitcoin price data from https://bitcoinition.com/.

2. Data Storage

The extension uses Chrome's storage API to save user preferences for the displayed badge metric and to cache the latest fetched data. This information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share any user data with third parties as we do not collect any personal information.

4. Security

While we implement reasonable security measures to protect the limited data stored by the extension, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at freebtcalerts@gmail.com.
